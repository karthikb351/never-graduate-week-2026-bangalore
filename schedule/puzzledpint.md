# Puzzled Pint Bangalore

Date: Tuesday, 12th May - 6:30pm - 11pm
Register: https://blr.puzzledpint.com

Puzzled Pint is a casual, social puzzle solving event which happens at bars/pubs on the second Tuesday of every month in multiple cities around the world. Recurse alums @karthikb351 and @kdqed run the Bangalore chapter(along with Non-Karthik Non-Recurse alum @psiderman). It's coincidentally it's happening during NGW, so people are invited to come participate. The event is walking distance from Underline Center at [Daddy's](https://maps.app.goo.gl/Cj3fTkBdf8qEK2LW7). It is absolutely free to attend, so consider coming by!
