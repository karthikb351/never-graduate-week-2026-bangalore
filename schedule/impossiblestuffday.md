# Impossible Stuff Day

Date: Monday, 11th May - 10am - 5pm at Underline Center

Contact: Unnati

Impossible Stuff Day is a special day in every RC batch where people attempt something that's ["hopelessly, foolishly ambitious"](https://www.jakelow.com/blog/reflecting-on-my-time-at-recurse-center#:~:text=hopelessly,%20foolishly%20ambitious%20to%20you) to them. The idea being that if you attempt something way outside of your current abilities, you tend to be surprised by how far you get, and you will learn a lot in the process. As part of our day to day lives, we don't usually get a chance to attempt things - and this is an excuse to do that and share it with others.
