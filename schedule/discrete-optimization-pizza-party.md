# Discrete optimization for the best pizza party

We'll learn about constraint optimization style of programming because the usual DSA isn't enough. We'll follow a the basic [modeling for discrete optimization coursera course](https://www.coursera.org/learn/basic-modeling) and the apply our learnings to solve [Farm frenzy pizza party](https://store.steampowered.com/app/38140/Farm_Frenzy_Pizza_Party/) with increasingly better modeling and complex cases.
